## What to Look for in a Budget Air Purifier

Not all sub-$150 purifiers are created equal. Here is what separates genuine value from wasted money:

**CADR rating is king.** Clean Air Delivery Rate is the single most objective measure of air purifier performance. Higher CADR means more clean air per minute. In this price range, aim for at least 140 CADR for bedrooms and 170+ for living rooms. Ignore marketing claims about "99% purification" that lack a CADR number to back them up.

**True HEPA vs. HEPA-type matters.** True HEPA (H13 or H14) captures 99.97% of particles at 0.3 microns. HEPA-type, HEPA-like, and HEPA-style filters have no standardized performance threshold and can be significantly less effective. Every purifier in our roundup uses genuine HEPA filtration — but many sub-$150 models on Amazon do not. Check the spec sheet carefully.

**Filter replacement cost is the hidden price tag.** A $90 purifier with $50/year filter costs is more expensive over three years than a $130 purifier with $30/year filters. Always look up replacement filter prices before buying. Here is the 3-year math for our picks:

| Purifier | Purchase | Annual Filters | Electricity | **3-Year Total** |
|----------|----------|---------------|-------------|-----------------|
| Levoit Vital 200S | $90 | $37-50 | $28 | **$229-268** |
| Levoit Core 300 | $100 | $30-40 | $24 | **$214-244** |
| Hathaspace HSP001 | $130 | $35 | $28 | **$263** |
| GermGuardian AC5250PT | $150 | $55-70 | $20 | **$335-380** |

**Room size matching prevents disappointment.** An air purifier rated for 200 sq. ft. will struggle in a 400 sq. ft. room. Match the CADR and coverage rating to your actual room size. If in doubt, size up — a purifier that is "too powerful" for your room simply cleans the air faster and can run on a quieter, lower setting.

**Smart features are no longer a luxury.** The Levoit Vital 200S proves that app control and air quality sensors belong in the sub-$100 category. Auto mode adjusts fan speed based on real-time PM2.5 readings, which means cleaner air with less noise and lower electricity costs. If smart features matter to you, the Vital 200S is the only sub-$150 pick that delivers them.
