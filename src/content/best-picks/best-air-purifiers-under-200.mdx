## What You Get Under $200

The sub-$200 category is surprisingly strong. Here is what you should expect — and what you give up compared to $200+ models:

**What you get:**
- True HEPA H13 filtration (99.97% particle capture)
- Activated carbon for odors
- CADR ratings of 140-246 — enough for rooms up to 360 sq. ft.
- Quiet operation suitable for bedrooms
- Washable pre-filters

**What you typically give up:**
- Smart app control (except the Levoit Core 400S at $220, just above our cutoff)
- Real-time PM2.5 sensors
- Premium build materials and design
- Very large room coverage (400+ sq. ft.)

For most people, the trade-offs are worth it. The air coming through a $160 HEPA filter is just as clean as air through a $580 HEPA filter.

## Total Cost of Ownership

The purchase price is only part of the equation. Filter replacements add $40-70 per year, and electricity adds $15-30. Here is the true 3-year cost:

| Purifier | Purchase | Yr 1 Filters | Yr 2 | Yr 3 | Electricity | **3-Year Total** |
|----------|----------|-------------|------|------|-------------|-----------------|
| Levoit Core 300 | $100 | $45 | $45 | $45 | $36 | **$271** |
| Winix 5500-2 | $160 | $54 | $54 | $54 | $45 | **$367** |
| Coway AP-1512HH | $229 | $40 | $40 | $40 | $40 | **$389** |

**Important context:** The Levoit Core 300 is cheapest but covers only 200 sq. ft. effectively. The Winix and Coway cover 360 sq. ft. — nearly double the room size. Per square foot of coverage, the Winix is actually the best value.

## When to Spend More

The sub-$200 category handles most needs, but consider spending more if:

- **Your room is 400+ sq. ft.** — Step up to the [Blueair 211+](/reviews/blueair-blue-pure-211-review) ($300) for 350 CADR
- **You want app control and PM2.5 data** — The [Levoit Core 400S](/reviews/levoit-core-400s-review) ($220) adds smart features
- **You have a very large open floor plan** — The [Dyson Big Quiet](/reviews/dyson-big-quiet-review) ($580) covers 1,000+ sq. ft.
- **You have formaldehyde concerns** — Only the Dyson offers catalytic formaldehyde destruction

For standard bedrooms and living rooms under 360 sq. ft., the sub-$200 purifiers deliver the same clean air as premium models.
