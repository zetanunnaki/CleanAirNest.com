## What $100 Actually Gets You

At this price point, you get genuine True HEPA filtration in a compact package. Here is a realistic expectations breakdown:

**What you get under $100:**
- True HEPA H13 filtration (99.97% particle capture at 0.3 microns)
- Activated carbon layer for light odor control
- Coverage for bedrooms, offices, and small living spaces (150-220 sq. ft.)
- Quiet operation suitable for overnight use
- 3-speed fan with optional timer

**What you give up:**
- Smart app control and Wi-Fi connectivity
- Real-time air quality sensors and auto mode
- Large room coverage (300+ sq. ft.)
- Washable HEPA filters (all budget models use replaceable filters)

The air passing through a $100 HEPA filter is just as clean as air through a $600 HEPA filter. You are paying more for coverage area, noise reduction, smart features, and build quality — not cleaner air.
