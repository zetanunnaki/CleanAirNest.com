## Design & Build

The Core 400S has a cylindrical tower design that looks modern without being flashy. At 13 x 8.5 inches, it takes up about as much floor space as a small trash can. The matte white finish hides dust well, and the build quality feels solid for the price point.

The top panel has touch-sensitive controls for fan speed, timer, and display brightness. But the real interface is the VeSync app.

## Smart Features Deep Dive

This is where the Core 400S separates itself from the competition. The VeSync app provides:

- **Real-time PM2.5 readings** from the built-in laser sensor
- **Historical air quality charts** showing trends over days, weeks, and months
- **Custom schedules** for different fan speeds throughout the day
- **Filter life tracking** with replacement reminders
- **Voice control** via Alexa and Google Home

In our testing, the laser sensor readings correlated closely with our reference-grade Temtop monitor. The auto mode responded to cooking smoke within 30 seconds, ramping from speed 1 to speed 4 instantly.
