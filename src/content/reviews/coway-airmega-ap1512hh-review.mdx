## Design & Build Quality

The Mighty has a clean, minimal aesthetic that blends into most rooms without drawing attention. At 16.8 x 18.3 x 9.6 inches, it is compact enough for a nightstand or bookshelf but substantial enough to feel well-built.

The front panel pops off easily for filter access — a small but appreciated design choice. The top-mounted controls are intuitive: fan speed, timer, ionizer toggle, and eco mode.

## Air Cleaning Performance

This is where the Coway Mighty truly earns its reputation. In our standardized testing:

- **PM2.5 reduction:** 98% in 30 minutes (350 sq. ft. room)
- **VOC reduction:** 85% in 60 minutes
- **Pollen simulation:** 99% reduction in 20 minutes

The four-stage filtration system is thorough. The washable pre-filter catches large particles like hair and pet fur, extending the life of the True HEPA filter behind it.

## Noise Levels

We measured noise at each fan speed:

| Speed | Noise Level | Comparable To |
|-------|------------|---------------|
| 1 (Low) | 24.4 dB | Whisper quiet |
| 2 (Medium) | 38.2 dB | Library |
| 3 (High) | 53.8 dB | Normal conversation |

On the lowest setting, the Mighty is practically inaudible. Even on high, it produces a smooth white noise that many users find pleasant for sleep.

## Running Costs

One of the Mighty's biggest advantages is its low total cost of ownership:

- **Energy consumption:** $1.50–$3.00/month (depending on usage)
- **HEPA filter replacement:** ~$40 every 12 months
- **Carbon filter replacement:** ~$18 every 6 months
- **Annual filter cost:** ~$76/year

This is significantly cheaper than most competitors in this price range.
