## Why hOmeLabs with Pump as a Replacement

The hOmeLabs 6,000 Sq Ft with Built-In Pump fills the same role as the discontinued Tosot: a pump-equipped dehumidifier for basements where upward drainage is needed. At 40 pints per day (104 pints max at saturation conditions), it handles large basements effectively, and the 6,000 sq. ft. coverage rating exceeds what the Tosot offered.

The built-in pump can push water vertically to a utility sink, window, or elevated drain — the same core functionality that made the Tosot popular.
