## What Makes It Different: HEPASilent Technology

Most air purifiers use traditional mechanical HEPA filtration — they force air through a dense filter mat. The Blueair 211+ takes a different approach with its proprietary HEPASilent technology.

HEPASilent combines electrostatic charging with mechanical filtration. Incoming particles are electrically charged before reaching the filter. Because charged particles are attracted to the filter fibers like magnets, the filter can use a less dense media while still achieving HEPA-level efficiency. The result is three key advantages:

1. **Higher airflow** at the same noise level (or lower noise at the same airflow)
2. **Lower energy consumption** — the motor doesn't need to work as hard
3. **Longer filter life** — less clogging than traditional dense HEPA media

## The Fabric Pre-Filter

The 211+ features colorful fabric pre-filters that wrap around the bottom intake. These serve two purposes:

1. **Aesthetics** — Available in multiple colors, they let you customize the look
2. **Pre-filtration** — They catch larger particles like pet hair and dust, extending the life of the main filter

The pre-filters are machine washable. We recommend washing them every 2-4 weeks, depending on how much pet hair and dust your home produces.
