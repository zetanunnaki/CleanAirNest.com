The Hey Dewy is not trying to humidify your whole bedroom. It is a personal humidifier designed to sit on your desk, nightstand, or hotel room table and keep the air immediately around your face hydrated. Created by a skincare brand, it targets beauty and skincare enthusiasts who know that dry air wreaks havoc on skin, but it has found a broader audience among travelers, office workers, and dorm residents.

At $39 with USB-C power, it goes wherever you go.

## Design & Build

The Hey Dewy looks like a miniature water bottle crossed with a lantern, available in several pastel colors. It measures just 4 x 4 x 7 inches and weighs about 10 ounces empty. The translucent tank holds 500 mL (about 17 ounces) of water, and the wide mouth opening makes it easy to fill from any faucet or water bottle.

The USB-C cable powers the unit from a laptop, power bank, wall adapter, or even a phone charger. There is no battery, so it runs only while plugged in — but USB-C power sources are everywhere now, making this limitation minor in practice. A single button cycles through three mist levels and off.

Build quality is surprisingly good for a $39 portable device. The matte soft-touch finish feels premium, and the tank seal is secure with no leaking during travel in our testing.

## Performance

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Tank capacity | 500 mL (17 oz) |
| Max mist output | ~80 mL/hr |
| Runtime (low) | ~10 hours |
| Runtime (high) | ~6 hours |
| Effective range | 3-4 foot radius |
| Auto shut-off | Yes, when tank is empty |

The Hey Dewy is a personal humidifier, not a room humidifier. In an open bedroom, it will not significantly change the overall humidity reading. Within a 3-4 foot radius — your desk area or the space around your pillow — it creates a noticeable zone of increased moisture. We measured a 6-8% humidity increase at 2 feet from the device in a 200 sq ft room, versus a 15-18% increase with a full-size humidifier like the MistAire.

For its intended use case — keeping your skin and nasal passages comfortable while you work at a desk or sleep — the Hey Dewy is effective. For humidifying an entire room, it is not powerful enough.

**Travel use:** The Hey Dewy excels here. The compact size fits in a carry-on, the 500 mL tank is refillable from any sink, and USB-C power means you can run it from your laptop in a hotel room. For frequent travelers who suffer from dry hotel air, this is a compelling solution.

## Noise Levels

| Setting | Noise Level |

The Hey Dewy is effectively silent at all settings. At low, it is quieter than ambient room noise in most environments. This makes it ideal for desktop use during video calls or for sleeping.

## Hey Dewy vs. Pure Enrichment MistAire

These serve different purposes. The MistAire is a proper room humidifier with a 1.5L tank and enough output to humidify a 200 sq ft bedroom. The Hey Dewy is a personal humidifier with a 500 mL tank that creates a comfort zone within arm's reach. If you need bedroom-wide humidity, buy the MistAire. If you need a portable, desk-friendly, or travel-ready device that keeps your immediate environment comfortable, the Hey Dewy fills a niche the MistAire cannot.

## The Verdict

The Hey Dewy is the best portable personal humidifier we have tested. It is not a replacement for a room humidifier, but it was never meant to be. For desk use, travel, dorm rooms, and skincare routines, the compact design, USB-C power, and silent operation make it uniquely practical. The $39 price is fair for the build quality and performance within its intended use case.

Buy it if you travel frequently, work at a desk all day, or want targeted humidity for your skin. Skip it if you need to humidify an entire room — the [MistAire](/reviews/pure-enrichment-mistaire-review) or [AquaOasis](/reviews/aquaoasis-cool-mist-review) are better for that.
