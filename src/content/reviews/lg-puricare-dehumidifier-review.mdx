## Why AEOCKY 80-Pint as a Replacement

The AEOCKY 80-Pint earned ENERGY STAR Most Efficient 2025 certification, which means it meets the highest energy efficiency standards set by the EPA — not just baseline Energy Star, but the top tier. For an 80-pint unit, that efficiency rating is notable. You get 60% more dehumidification capacity than the old LG PuriCare while spending less on both the unit itself and monthly electricity.

The intelligent humidistat lets you set a target humidity level and the unit adjusts fan speed automatically. Smart controls allow scheduling and monitoring, though the app ecosystem is not as mature as LG ThinQ was.

## Performance & Coverage

At 80 pints per day, this unit handles spaces the LG PuriCare could not. Large basements, multi-room areas, and homes with persistent moisture problems benefit from this capacity. The 4,500 sq. ft. coverage rating means a single unit can serve most residential basements and main floors.

The included drain hose enables continuous operation without emptying a bucket — connect it to a floor drain or utility sink and leave it running. There is no built-in pump, so you will need gravity drainage or an external condensate pump for upward drainage scenarios.

## Noise & Daily Use

At 48 dB, the AEOCKY is louder than the LG PuriCare was (44 dB). That 4 dB difference is noticeable in a quiet room. For a basement or utility space, 48 dB will not be an issue — it sounds like a quiet refrigerator. For a bedroom or living room, consider whether that noise level works for your setup.

The unit has a larger physical footprint than 50-pint models, which is expected given the increased capacity. Plan for a dedicated floor spot near a drainage point for the best results.

## AEOCKY 80-Pint vs. GE ADEL45LY

The GE ADEL45LY is a 25-pint unit designed for smaller spaces at a lower price ($229). The AEOCKY delivers over three times the capacity (80 vs 25 pints) and covers significantly more area (4,500 vs 1,500 sq. ft.). The GE offers Wi-Fi via SmartHQ, a more established brand name, and a smaller footprint. Choose the AEOCKY for large or very damp spaces; choose the GE for moderate humidity in bedrooms and garages under 1,500 sq. ft.
