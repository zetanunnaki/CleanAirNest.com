## Why AEOCKY 80-Pint Smart as a Replacement

The AEOCKY 80-Pint Smart Dehumidifier is a significant upgrade over the discontinued Vremi in every measurable way. At 80 pints per day versus the Vremi's 50, it offers 60% more dehumidification capacity. The intelligent humidistat, ENERGY STAR Most Efficient 2025 certification, and smart controls make it a more capable and efficient unit — all at essentially the same $240 price point.

## Performance

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Moisture removal | 80 pints/day |
| Coverage area | Up to 4,500 sq ft |
| Built-in pump | No |
| Continuous drain | Yes (gravity drain hose) |
| Min operating temp | 41°F |
| Smart controls | Yes (intelligent humidistat) |

The AEOCKY's 80-pint capacity handles large basements, open-concept spaces, and severe moisture problems that would have overwhelmed the Vremi's 50-pint rating. The intelligent humidistat maintains target humidity efficiently without overcycling, reducing energy consumption and extending compressor life.

ENERGY STAR Most Efficient 2025 certification confirms this is among the most efficient high-capacity dehumidifiers available — a meaningful advantage for a unit that may run 12-24 hours per day during humid months.

Like the Vremi, the AEOCKY does not include a built-in pump. You get a gravity drain port for continuous drainage downward. For upward drainage, you will need an external condensate pump ($30-50).

## Noise Levels

At 48 dB, the AEOCKY operates at the same noise level the Vremi did — comparable to a quiet refrigerator. For a unit with 60% more capacity, maintaining the same noise floor is impressive. It is audible in a quiet room but easy to tune out within minutes.
